About Campus Exchange

Connecting Students for Sustainable Second-Hand Buying & Selling

Our Mission

Campus Exchange was created to provide students with a **safe, easy, and sustainable way** to buy and sell second-hand items within their university communities.

By connecting students directly, we aim to reduce waste, save money, and make sure great items find a new home instead of ending up in landfills.

How It Works

  • ✔ Browse Listings: Search for second-hand items available within your campus.
  • ✔ Post Your Own Items: List your unused items for sale and help another student in need.
  • ✔ Connect & Exchange: Chat securely with buyers/sellers and arrange a convenient meet-up.
