About Campus Exchange

Connecting Students Through Sustainable Second-Hand Buying & Selling

Our Mission

Campus Exchange is built to empower students with a safe, convenient, and eco-friendly platform for buying and selling second-hand items within their campus network.

We’re committed to reducing waste, helping students save money, and ensuring that useful goods find new homes instead of landfills.

How It Works

  • 🛍️ Browse Listings: Discover second-hand items available on your campus.
  • 📤 Create a Listing: Post items you no longer need using a simple form.
  • 📍 Set Location Pin: Use Google Maps to precisely pin your item's meetup spot.
  • 💬 Contact & Meet: Message buyers/sellers to arrange safe meetups on campus.

User Guide & Controls

  • ✅ Search: Use the search bar to find listings by keywords or tags (e.g. #bike, #textbook).
  • ✅ Filters: Filter listings by distance using the dropdown on the homepage map.
  • ✅ Map View: Listings are displayed on a map to help you find nearby deals.
  • ✅ Creating Listings: Click “Create a Listing” in the nav bar, fill out the form, and upload images.
  • ✅ Location Pin: After entering a location, use the “Set Pin” button to precisely place your item on the map.
  • ✅ Manage Listings: View and edit your active listings under “Manage Your Listings.”
